ABOUT MDF

Mushroom Development Foundation (MDF) is a not for profit citizen based organization started in 1994 in finding livelihood based solutions from agricultural waste and providing nutritional food security to the nation.

READ MORE !

Objective

The objective of the project is to resolve the gaps existing today in scaling up the mushroom cultivation with an innovative approach so that the activity becomes sustainable beyond the project implementation period as mentioned below:

To bring in Awareness on mushroom consumption in the city and awareness on cultivation through the weekly markets in the rural areas and daily markets in the urban areas by using practical demonstration audio, video, print publicity materials etc.

At the weekly rural market mushroom spawn, Technical guidance, Life demonstration on mushroom production. Mushroom Houses etc. will be exhibited and all required planting materials etc, will be made available.

Assuring continuous supply of quality spawn to the participating farmers in collaboration with all concern project.Organize community governance mechanism to manage collection and distribution system for sustainable growth.

Organize farmers by teaming up for doing business with an additional livelihood opportunity from available agriculture waste by producing mushrooms.Effective handholding for sustainable growth of the mushroom industry for the participants through the FDU
